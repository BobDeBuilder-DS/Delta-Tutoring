What makes Delta different

Two-sided vetting

We assess both tutors and students before any match is made. Tutors must demonstrate they can teach, not just solve. Students must show they are ready to commit.

Subject-matched pairs

Your tutor has studied the exact module you need help with. No generalists. Engineering maths, statistics, calculus — matched precisely.

Progress over marks

We track growth from your baseline. A student moving from 38% to 62% tells a better story than one coasting at 70%. Delta measures change — that's the point.

Tutor protection

Our tutors are capped at 20 hours per month. They are students too. We protect their academic performance so they show up at their best for you.

Monthly packages

All packages are valid for the current calendar month only. Unused hours do not carry over.

How it works

01

You apply

Fill in a short intake form. Tell us your module, faculty, year level, and where you're struggling.

02

We assess fit

A brief intake conversation to understand your needs, preparation level, and commitment. Not every applicant is accepted, and that's by design.

03

We match you

You're paired with a tutor who has studied your exact module at your institution or equivalent.

04

You commit and grow

Sessions begin. We track your Δ, your change from baseline, monthly. Accountability runs both ways.
